Hi! 👋

I am a tenure-track research scientist at the Technologies of Vision Lab of the Bruno Kessler Foundation in Trento, Italy. My research interests are in 3D perception and understanding, with a focus on object 6D pose estimation. I have been a pioneer in the field of Geometric deep learning.

If you want to know more about me, you can have a look at my cv or at my LinkedIn page.
